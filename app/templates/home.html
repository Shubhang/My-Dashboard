What is MY Dashboard?

MY Dashboard is a result of a collaboration between EY and the Penn State Nittany Data Labs (NDL). Started in early 2020, this project was designed by Greg Glatzer, Jordan Williard, and Shubhang Sharma along with the guidance of Ryan Ward. Throughout the year, we would meet on a weekly or bi-weekly basis to develop the project to what you see today.

What does MY Dashboard do?

The objective of this project is to build a tool that aids consultants in their day-to-day interactions with the clients they are working with. The vision is that consultants can log on to the dashboard and type in the name of the company they are going to be meeting with that day and get an overview of what is happening in the world relevant to that company. The dashboard generated will provide the user with many insightful pieces of information, including statistics related to stocks for the week, an AI-driven sentiment vs. time graph to help predict stock prices, and law compliance summaries relevant to the company. To get a better understanding of how to use the tool and all it offers, see the video below.

In addition to catering to the individual user, MY Dashboard also presents functionality for working in a team. The “Create Group” tab allows users to add companies to a group and share the group link for other users to view and edit.

We hope you enjoy exploring our tool that we put so much work into! We enjoyed learning how to create MY Dashboard, and hope that it accomplishes the task we set out to achieve. Enjoy!
